Slide 1 – Title & Purpose

PubRun San Antonio: “Waldo Night” Growth Campaign

Presented by Axel Colunga – Timeless Solutions

  • Goal: Increase engagement, build following, and position PubRun San Antonio as the city’s go-to monthly nightlife event.
  • This embedded deck outlines the full strategy, content plan, and funnel system to turn in-person energy into online growth.

Slide 2 – Key Insights

  • Happens every First Friday of the Month (monthly event).
  • Around 100 attendees per night already — strong foundation to build from.
  • Free entry through Eventbrite (great FOMO trigger).
  • Starting point: “New Friends” bar under McDonald’s downtown.
  • Uses Eventbrite check-in and wristbands for bar access.
  • December theme already confirmed: Bad Santa PubRun.

Slide 3 – The Core Challenge

  • The event is vibrant in real life, but the digital presence doesn’t capture that energy.
  • Few attendees tag PubRun SA online.
  • Little consistency between events.
  • Social media lacks a cohesive voice and doesn’t trigger “I need to be there” FOMO.
  • Objective: convert that real-world buzz into constant online visibility.

Slide 4 – The Vision

“If people see it, they’ll want to be in it.”
  • Transform PubRun SA into a monthly visual story that people can’t ignore.
  • Every run should leave behind a trail of posts, tags, and stories.
  • Establish a sustainable content system: hype before, fun during, nostalgia after.
  • Outcome: community growth, partnerships, and regional expansion.

Slide 5 – Campaign 1: “Waldo’s Missing Countdown”

  • Launch 10 days before the event (Halloween → Waldo Night).
  • Daily teasers building hype:
  • Days 10–7: “Waldo Spotted” clues and hints about locations.
  • Days 6–3: Outfit ideas and costume inspiration.
  • Days 2–1: AI-generated crowd videos and teaser edits.
  • Day 0: Final “It’s Waldo Night” hype video and story posts.

Consistency is key: 10 days = 10 posts = algorithm dominance.

Slide 6 – Campaign 2: “Waldo Spotted Here 👀” Bar Collaborations

  • Each participating bar posts: “Waldo spotted at [Bar Name] 👀”.
  • PubRun SA co-posts the same content using Instagram collaboration.
  • Format: short photo carousels (5–6 images per bar).
  • 9–10 bars = 9–10 organic collaborations = 9–10 exposure spikes.
  • Benefits: cross-audience growth, bar relationships, constant story sharing.

Slide 7 – Campaign 3: “Before / After PubRun” Booth

  • Low-cost, reusable photo-spot concept.
  • Props/signs: “POV Before PubRun” and “POV After PubRun.”
  • Encourages attendees to tag @PubRunSanAntonio in posts and stories.
  • Helps casual guests (with 500–2k followers) generate organic FOMO.
  • Each tagged story = new eyeballs + built-in proof that people had fun.

Slide 8 – Campaign 4: Influencer Activation

  • At start of the event: ask crowd who has 5k+ followers.
  • Offer: “Free shot for anyone who posts and tags both the bar and PubRun SA.”
  • You (or the content team) can film their videos or TikToks for them.
  • Provide wristbands labeled “Official PubRun Creator.”
  • Purpose: make micro-influencers feel special while spreading reach organically.

Slide 9 – Funnel System

Pre-Event Funnel

  • Guests scan QR code or Eventbrite check-in.
  • Option to “Add PubRun SA to Home Screen.”
  • Offer: sign up now and claim a free/discounted drink next month.

Next-Month Funnel

  • On return, they redeem the ticket for a welcome shot or drink.
  • Bars benefit (guaranteed traffic).
  • PubRun SA benefits (guaranteed attendance and data capture).

Slide 10 – Future Content Concepts

  • AI-generated teaser clips showing crowds, neon, energy.
  • Carousel posts of “Top Outfits of the Night.”
  • UGC reposts of attendees holding “Before/After” signs.
  • Story series: “Where was Waldo spotted tonight?”
  • Optional short skits with runners or bartenders for humor + virality.

Slide 11 – Expansion Vision

  • Turn PubRun SA into a scalable model for El Paso, Denver, and Florida.
  • Consistent monthly system = franchise-ready event framework.
  • Each city adopts the same strategy with localized flair.

Monetization channels:

  • Sponsorships (breweries, local brands).
  • Merch (shirts, wristbands, costumes).
  • VIP skip-the-line passes.
  • Affiliate deals with bars.

  • Approve deliverables + budget.
  • Begin content creation (AI videos, countdown assets, and collabs).
